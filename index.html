About Nails by biscuit

I'm Vickie. I drink a mental amount of tea. My home always smells like nail polish. My nickname is Biscuits (or Bear...depends who you ask). I live for all things colourful and sparkly. I am slave to glitter and studs. There is nothing that a veggie brunch can't cure.

I am based in deepest, darkest South East London but I will travel to you, provided you don't live on Mars.

All the designs you see here can be altered to suit you. Shout me with your ideas and we'll make it work.
